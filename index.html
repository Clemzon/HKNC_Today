Welcome to HKNC Today

HKNC Today is a student-focused campus website designed to make it easier to check what is happening around HKNC. Instead of searching through emails, flyers, conversations, and scattered notices, this site brings important updates together into one simple place.

Think of this homepage as a front page for campus life. It offers a quick look at news, highlights, meals, activities, and helpful links, with full details available on the rest of the site.

Latest News

  • Cook of the Month Recognition

    A fellow participant has been recognized as the latest Cook of the Month in Independent Living. Visit the Cook of the Month page to read the spotlight and celebrate the achievement.

  • New Brew in Town

    There is a new coffee option available in the residential lounge. Along with the regular K-Cup machine, residential staff has now added an espresso pod machine for students and participants to enjoy.

    The machine is located in the lounge area across from the main office. If you enter the lounge from the stairs, make an immediate right and continue past the two recliners. You will find a sink area. The brew machine is on the counter to the right of the sink.

    To use it, choose the pod you want, place it in the machine, select your cup size, and press start. After a short moment, your coffee will be ready. The espresso pods can be found in a drawer directly below the machine.

    If you need help, feel free to ask a staff member. They will be happy to show you how to use the machine.

    Thank you to Kathy, Chris and the residential staff for adding another nice feature to the student lounge.

  • Easter Sunday Service Invitation

    Participants are invited to join an Easter Sunday service shared as an optional opportunity for anyone who would like to take part.

    Shelter Rock Church: The online service will be available by First livestream at 9:30 - 10:45 AM and the second ervice at 11:30 - 12:45 AM, with the on-demand video starting at 12:00 PM.

    Those who plan to join can use the church’s YouTube page to access the livestream or watch the on-demand service later in the day.

    We hope everyone who would like to participate has a chance to join and enjoy the service.
