  • Spring Has Arrived in New York: Warmer Days Are Finally Here!

    After making it through a blizzard and a long, harsh winter, spring has finally arrived in New York. The cold days are beginning to fade, and the campus is ready to welcome warmer weather, fresh air, and more time outdoors. As the season changes, we can all start looking forward to outdoor activities, walks, and enjoying the beauty of spring around us. With New York spring comes new energy, new opportunities, and a chance to get outside after spending so much time indoors through the winter months. Of course, spring near a nature preserve also means one thing: mosquitoes. As the weather warms up, it is a good idea to be prepared and take a few extra steps to stay comfortable while enjoying the outdoors. Even with a few seasonal challenges, spring is a welcome change, and we are ready to enjoy everything it brings.
