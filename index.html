Welcome to HKNC Today

HKNC Today is designed to give students and participants one central, student-friendly place to find useful campus information. Instead of searching through multiple emails, flyers, conversations, or notices, this website is meant to make important updates easier to access and easier to understand.

The goal is simple: create one place where students can quickly check what is happening around campus and find the information they need without unnecessary confusion.

Why This Website Exists

HKNC Today is being built to give students a single, accessible place to find useful campus information. The website is intended to reduce confusion, improve access to updates, and make it easier for students to stay informed about meals, activities, support resources, and student involvement opportunities.

Accessibility is a major part of this effort. The website should stay clear, organized, readable, and usable for students who rely on screen readers and other assistive technology.

Latest News

  • New Brew in Town

    There is a new coffee option available in the residential lounge. Along with the regular K-Cup machine, residential staff has now added an espresso pod machine for students and participants to enjoy.

    The machine is located in the lounge area across from the main office. If you enter the lounge from the stairs, make an immediate right and continue past the two recliners. You will find a sink area. The brew machine is on the counter to the right of the sink.

    To use it, choose the pod you want, place it in the machine, select your cup size, and press start. After a short moment, your coffee will be ready. The espresso pods can be found in a drawer directly below the machine.

    If you need help, feel free to ask a staff member. They will be happy to show you how to use the machine.

    Thank you to Kathy and the residential staff for adding another nice feature to the student lounge.
