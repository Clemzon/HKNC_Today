Suggestions & Campus Ideas

Share ideas, improvements, and concerns that may help improve student life around campus.

About This Page

This page gives students a simple way to share suggestions, ideas, and feedback about campus life. Suggestions may be reviewed by staff, student leadership, or the Town Hall Committee depending on the topic.

This page is intended for constructive suggestions such as activity ideas, dining feedback, accessibility improvements, student life concerns, and general campus improvement ideas.

How It Works

  1. Open the suggestion form.
  2. Choose the area your suggestion is about.
  3. Describe your suggestion clearly.
  4. Staff or committee members review submissions.
  5. Appropriate suggestions may be discussed or acted on later.

Privacy & Safety

Your privacy matters. Please do not include personal medical, financial, or other sensitive private information in your suggestion.

If the Microsoft Form is configured for anonymous submissions, your name will not be collected. If identification is required later, that should be clearly explained on the form itself.

This page is not for emergencies or urgent safety issues. For urgent matters, please contact the appropriate staff or campus support office directly.

Examples of Suggestion Topics

  • Meal ideas or dining improvements
  • Weekend or evening activity suggestions
  • Accessibility improvements around campus
  • Student common area ideas
  • Technology or equipment concerns
  • General quality-of-life improvements
