What is Creative Corner?

Creative Corner is a campus showcase for participants who want to share what they make, create, build, write, cook, design, or promote. This page is meant to celebrate student effort, talent, and growth across different departments and personal interests.

Some participants may want to share a dish they prepared in Independent Living. Others may want to highlight an art project, a written article, a YouTube channel, a tech project, or other creative work. Creative Corner gives those efforts a place to be seen and appreciated by peers and staff.

How to Share Your Work

  1. Create something you would like others to experience.
  2. Submit your work: Talk with your instructor about submitting your work to HKNC Today, to share with everyon!
  3. All work must be submitted tothe instructor before posting.
  4. Once approved, it can be featured here for the campus community.

Submissions may include food showcases, art projects, written work, videos, audio, design work, or other creative content, depending on how the page is managed.

Why Creative Corner Matters

Creative Corner gives participants a place to be seen, supported, and encouraged by their peers. It helps highlight growth, creativity, independence, and the pride people take in the things they make.

It also gives departments a place to spotlight participant work in a way that helps build confidence and community connection across campus.

Guidelines

  • Keep submissions respectful and school-appropriate.
  • Do not include highly personal or private information.
  • Only submit work you created or have permission to share.
  • Submissions may be reviewed before being posted.
  • Photos, names, and identifying details should only be shared with approval.
  • Accessibility and participant safety should remain priorities.
