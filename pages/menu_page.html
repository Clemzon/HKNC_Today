Menu Overview

This page is designed to support the new weekly menu system. Staff can maintain the weekly menu through Microsoft services, while students can use this page to review the full week or focus on a single day.

A separate daily publishing system may be added later. For now, the daily view on this page comes from the weekly menu display.
