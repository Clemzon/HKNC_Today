What is Peer-2-Peer?

Peer-2-Peer is a student-centered space where students can offer help or request help from other students. This page is designed to make it easier to connect over shared skills, study support, language practice, and everyday campus life.

Whether you are strong in Braille, fluent in ASL, comfortable with technology, or looking for a study partner, this is the place to start.

How to Post

  1. Choose whether you are offering help or requesting help.
  2. Complete the submission form.
  3. Your post will be reviewed before appearing on the page.
  4. Once approved, other students can view your post and respond.

Posts may include tutoring offers, study support, language practice, technology help, campus navigation support, and other peer-based help.

Examples of What You Can Post

  • I can help with beginner Braille.
  • I am looking for a study partner for class.
  • I can help with ASL fingerspelling practice.
  • I need help learning iPhone accessibility settings.
  • I speak Spanish and can help with translation.
  • I am looking for someone to practice technology skills with.

Guidelines

  • Keep posts respectful and school-appropriate.
  • Only submit genuine offers for help or requests for help.
  • Do not include highly personal or private information.
  • Posts may be reviewed before they are shared.
  • Posts may be removed after they expire or are no longer needed.
